Our Mission

Today's media and news channels bombard people with mostly negative news consummed on the dayly by millions of people. At Souly4Good we think otherwise. We believe in delivering happiness and inspiration to people's lives by focusing on our three core values:

Positive News

Curated news content garantying positive news and solution oriented coverage to current worlwide problems.

Helping you reconnect with your inner hero

How many times a week do we sit and complain about a bad situation but yet we fail to see what we can actually do to change the situation... This is why we added the Challenge feature to help me and you connect with others who care in our local communities so that we together can have a positive impact in the World.

About

Souly4Good emerged as an idea to offer something else than the typical negative news served on the daily by media. Souly4Good is centered on spreading the joy of life in a social media platform designed to help connect people around random acts of kindness and shine light on everyday heroes.
